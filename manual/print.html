Print your image

To print your image from this app, you can choose the File > Print menu in the top menu bar or press command + P. The print dialog will open, with a preview of your printed document. You can adjust the printing options if needed. At last, you can click the Print button to start printing.

To get more information about printing, please refer to the Print documents from your Mac.

You can scale the image to fit your printer’s paper. See Reduce or enlarge pages and images to fit on a page

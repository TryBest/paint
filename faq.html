How to add curved texts?

There are two ways to add curved texts.

  • Add curved texts directly on the canvas.

    1. Please choose the Object > Add Curved Texts... menu on the top menu bar.
    2. In the dialog that appears, you can choose a path for the curved texts and then click the OK button.
    3. Type the texts you want to add.
    4. Click the esc key on the keyboard to finish typing.
    5. Adjust the path and shape of the curved texts as you want if needed.

  • Add curved text on to existing shape or path.

    1. Please select an existing shape or path on the canvas that you want to add curved texts to.

    Tips

    If there isn't such a shape and path or you don't want to use an exiting shape and path, please choose the curve line tool, broken line tool, ellipse tool or vector pen tool on the toolbar to add a new shape or path.
    You can delete the new added shape or path after you finish adding curved texts if you want.

Why no new app is installed after purchasing the full-featured version?

When your purchase the full-featured version in this app, the app will remove all limitations, enable all features and become the full-featured version automatically. It won't install another app. You can continue to use this app with all features.

I purchased the full-featured version. Why many menu commands and features are still greyed out and disabled?

Some menu commands and features are specific to the selected objects or image part. So they are only available when some objects or some part of an image are selected. You can select some objects or some part of an image on the canvas and then those menu commands will be enabled.

I purchased the full-featured version. But the app is still the free version. How to restore my purchase?

Maybe your purchase receipt is lost on your current Mac. Please delete this app from your Mac and then re-install it from App Store. Your purchase will be restored automatically.
It this still won't work. Please choose the Help > Feedback Issues... menu on the top menu bar to create a report and then send it to us by email support@trybestapps.com.

Tips

You should use the same Apple ID with which you purchased the full-featured version when re-installing this app, or your purchase can't be restored.

How to apply effects and filters to some part of an image?

  1. Step 1: Please choose a selection tool and select an image part which you want with it.
  2. Step 2: Choose an effect or a filter.
  • You can choose the Object > Effect menu on the top menu bar to choose an effect or a filter.
  • You can also right click the selected image part to show the context menu and then select the Effect menu to choose an effect or a filter.
